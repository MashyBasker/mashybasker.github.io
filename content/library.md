These are links to things and people I find interesting, useful or beautiful.


### Cool / Useful

- [dingboard](https://dingboard.com/): best meme editor out there
- [panelsdesu](https://panelsdesu.com/): great for searching manga panels
- [grep.app](https://grep.app/): grep over the entire github database
- [texnique](https://texnique.xyz/): gamify learning latex
- [anilist](https://anilist.co/): goodreads for anime/manga

### People

- [Nicholas Carlini](https://nicholas.carlini.com/): Research Scientist at Google DeepMind specializing in adversarial techniques for ML systems. Writes insightful blogs.
- [Dmitrii Kovanikov](https://x.com/ChShersh): Huge OCaml & FP advocate. Writes interesting blogs on category theory (I haven't read them though).
- [Vivek Tejuja](https://x.com/vivekisms): Bibliophile and author. I especially enjoy reading his book reviews..
- [Anu Atluru](https://anuatluru.com/): Writes thought-provoking essays on life, startups and more.
- [Shashank Dixit](https://x.com/protosphinx): Mostly known as Sphinx online. Shares some solid takes on Twitter.
- [George Hotz](https://x.com/realGeorgeHotz): Hacker, founder of tinygrad and comma ai. Someday I'd love to work with him.
- [Kailash Nadh](https://nadh.in/): CTO at Zerodha and founder of FOSS United, working on some really cool side projects. Definitely someone I want to work with.
- [Fabrice Bellard](https://bellard.org/): Creator of QEMU, FFmpeg, TCC and much more. In my opinion, one of the most important computer scientists.
- [Andrew Kelley](https://andrewkelley.me/): Creator of Zig, libsoundio and more. 
- [Mitchell Hashimoto](https://mitchellh.com/): Ex-CEO, Ex-CTO and co-founder of HashiCorp. Creator of Ghostty. Many of my programming philosophies have been influenced by him.
